Rules & Scoring
Read before you play
All scores are based on 90 minutes + stoppage time. If a knockout game goes to extra time, those goals count. If it goes straight to a penalty shootout with no extra time goals, the score is locked at the 90-min result. Penalties are excluded entirely from scoring.
Predictions lock automatically 1 hour before kickoff. Once locked, no changes are possible.
